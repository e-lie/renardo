## Quick Customization Guide

Most customization options in Renardo are accessible through:

1. The settings menu in the top navigation bar
2. The theme selector in the bottom status bar
3. Direct manipulation of panels by dragging dividers

Your customization preferences are saved automatically between sessions, ensuring your workspace remains exactly as you left it.
